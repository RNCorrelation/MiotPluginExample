WHAT INFORMATION IS COLLECTED AND HOW DO WE USE IT?

TYPES OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

In order to provide our services to you, we will ask you to provide personal information that is necessary to provide those services to you. If you do not provide your personal information, we may not be able to provide you with our products or services.

We may collect the following types of information (which may or may not be personal information):

Information you provide to us or upload (including your contact details): We may collect any and all personal information you provide to us, like your Mi Account details.

Device data: We may gather and use data related to your device, such as the type of device, product model information, product device ID, IP address, and MAC address in order to provide device-specific services and to diagnose and solve problems reported by users.

Wi-Fi information:We may collect and use information relating to your Wi-Fi connection, such as SSID, signal strength, and information which needs to be gathered in order to connect to Wi-Fi and use web services.

Air conditioner information: We may collect and use information relating to your air conditioner, such as the air conditioner's brand, power consumption, on/off status, temperature setting, mode, power level, and oscillation status in order to display the status of your air conditioner in the app.

User scenarios: We may collect and use saved information needed to provide user scenario functionality.

Device logs:We may collect device logs, such as user crash logs, as well as information relating to how users operate their device in order to provide a better product and app user experience.
